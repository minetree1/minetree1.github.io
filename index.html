Server FAQs.

1. What is a Lifesteal server?
A Lifesteal server is a unique Minecraft experience where you steal health from other players upon defeating them. Each time you kill another player, you gain their health (hearts), but beware – others can do the same to you! It's an intense survival mode with a focus on PvP battles and strategy.
2. How do I join the server?
To join MineTree, simply open Minecraft, select Multiplayer, and enter our server IP: mc.minetree.xyz. Hit "Join Server" and you're all set to play!
3. Can I play solo or do I have to join a team?
You can absolutely play solo if you prefer! However, teaming up with other players can be a great way to strengthen your position and protect yourself. It’s entirely up to you whether you want to go it alone or form alliances.
4. Is there a whitelist or application process?
No, our server is open to everyone! Simply connect with the server IP and you'll be able to play immediately.
5. How do the Lifesteal mechanics work?
Every time you kill another player, you gain hearts (health). The more players you defeat, the stronger you become. However, if you die, you lose hearts, so make sure to protect yourself and keep your health high!
